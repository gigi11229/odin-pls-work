A simple, easy to make recipe for vanilla cake!

Ingredients

  • 4 Large eggs
  • 2 1/2 Cups flour
  • 1 Cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 Cups Granulated sugar
  • 1 Cup buttermilk
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 4 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 Tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350˚F, flour two 9" cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together all dry ingredients.
  3. Beat butter and sugar until thick and fluffy.
  4. add 4 eggs, beating well with each addition. Add vanilla extract and combine.
  5. Add flour mixture and combine until batter is smooth.
  6. Divide batter into cake pans evenly and Bake at 350˚F for 28-39 minutes.
  7. Apply frosting when cooled.
