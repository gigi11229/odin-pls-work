A simple recipe for chocolate chip cookies!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 Cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 Cups granulated sugar
  • 1 Cup butter
  • 1 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Cups chocolate chips
  • 3/4 Cups brown sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375Â°F. Mix together dry ingredients into a bowl
  2. Beat softened butter and sugars together until fluffy
  3. Beat in egg and Vanilla extract until smooth. Stir in dry ingredients until blended.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips. Roll dough into tablespoon sized balls and place on cookie sheet.
  5. Bake 8 to 10 minutes until light brown around the edges. Let cool for 2 minutes.
